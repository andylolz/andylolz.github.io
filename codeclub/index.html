Introduction

Today, let’s have a go publishing our own news stories on the web!

Step 1: How do websites work?

Activity Checklist

  • On this page, try clicking View -> Developer -> View Source. Can you understand it?
  • It’s called “HTML”. That’s the language Chrome (and other web browsers) understand.
  • Try doing the same thing on the newsround website. What do you think?

Step 2: Put on your X-Ray Goggles!

Because HTML is just some text, we can change it and save it.

Activity Checklist

  • Open X-Ray Goggles and follow the instructions on the page. Can you change the background?
  • Try changing some text on the page, too.

Step 3: Get set up…

Activity Checklist

  • Log in by clicking “Sign In”, then “log in with Persona”, then enter: (the password is the usual one!)
  • Then add X-Ray Goggles to Chrome by going here and dragging the yellow bookmarklet to the bookmark bar. Ask Andy if you get stuck!

Step 4: Time to make some news!

Activity Checklist

  • Last month, David Cameron won the general election. Here’s a news story about it.
  • Can you use X-Ray goggles to change this news story, so it’s about your school elections? Or make the story about something else?
  • Go to the page, click the bookmarklet, and then change the news!
  • When you are done, click Publish to save your new version.
